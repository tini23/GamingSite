Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Stunning graphics: The PS5’s custom GPU offers remarkable visuals with ray tracing, making games look incredibly realistic..
  • Lightning-fast load times: The built-in SSD drastically reduces load times, enhancing the overall gaming experience.
  • DualSense controller: The new controller offers haptic feedback and adaptive triggers, providing a more immersive experience in games.
  • Exclusive games: PlayStation is known for its strong lineup of exclusive titles, like Astro-Bot, Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart, Spider-Man 2 and God Of War : Ragnarok.
  • Backward compatibility: Most of your favorite PS4 games are playable on the PS5, ensuring a seamless transition.

Cons

  • Storage limitations: While the SSD is fast, the 1TB storage can fill up quickly, especially with the large sizes of next-gen games. External drives are needed for extra space.
  • No native 1440p support: While the PS5 supports 4K, it doesn’t support 1440p natively, which can be a downside for gamers with 1440p monitors.
